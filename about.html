About

A home for businesses hardworking businesses.

Kilonova Holdings is a private South African holding company. We take equity stakes in operating businesses and, rather than managing a fund with a fixed exit date, remain actively invested.

Philosophy

We can do great things together.

Most of the businesses we're interested in aren't glamorous. They sell, service, or maintain the equipment that other industries — manufacturing, food processing, agriculture — rely on every day. That kind of business rewards operators who understand it and owners who don't rush it.

We back management teams to keep running their businesses their way. Kilonova's role is to provide resources, whatever they mat be, and a longer time horizon than most buyers offer, with a second set of hands for the decisions that benefit from an outside view — funding growth, structuring a deal, or bringing in the right supplier relationship.

Sectors of interest

Where we look

Industrial equipment

Sales, rental, and service of equipment used on industrial and construction sites.

Asset care & maintenance

Businesses that keep other companies' plant and equipment running — inspection, servicing, and asset-care programmes.

Process industries

Suppliers and service providers into sugar, food processing, and other continuous-process manufacturing.

Adjacent opportunities

We're open to businesses outside these categories where the same operator quality and demand fundamentals apply.

Structure

How Kilonova is organised

Kilonova Holdings (Pty) Ltd sits independantly from its operating companies as a holding entity. Each business — currently — operates independently under its own brand and management, with Kilonova holding an equity stake at the holding-company level.
