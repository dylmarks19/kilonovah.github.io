Thesis

We hold what we'd be proud to run.

Kilonova Holdings takes controlling and minority stakes in South African businesses with real operating discipline — companies that sell, service, or support equipment other industries depend on. We're not a fund with a fixed horizon; we're a permanent home for businesses that need patient ownership and room to compound.

Every holding keeps its own name, its own team, and its own way of working. What we bring is capital, governance, and a longer view.
